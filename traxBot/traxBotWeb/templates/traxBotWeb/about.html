About traxBot

The goal of traxBot is to porivde a robot vacuum cleaner that is unencumbered by stairs, a feature common in many households.

My design is loosely based upon that of other robot vacuum cleaners, with several key distinctions.

Instead of wheels for mobility, I have opted for treads, as they provide a more easily manufacurable mobility system for traversing stairs.

In addition to the randomized clearing pattern achieved by merely changing drive direction when an obstacle is detected, the traxBot will have the capability to map your household and determine the most efficient algorithm for clearing said household.

As the creator of traxBot, I firmly believe that your personal data should remain private, and it is my goal in the creation of this machine to ensure that the data of your household remains within it, as much as possible

To do this, all computation will occur client-side, through a raspberry pi in the traxBot, that will hopefully minimiize the exposure of your valuable, personal data.
